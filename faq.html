Products & Quality

Research peptides are short chains of amino acids used in scientific and laboratory settings to study biological processes, receptor interactions, and physiological mechanisms. They are intended exclusively for qualified researchers, scientists, and laboratory professionals conducting in-vitro studies. They are not for human or animal consumption.
Every batch is sent to an accredited third-party laboratory for HPLC (High-Performance Liquid Chromatography) analysis and mass spectrometry (MS) confirmation. These tests verify both purity percentage and molecular identity. A Certificate of Analysis (CoA) is available for each product and batch.
Yes, absolutely. CoAs are available upon request for any product in our catalogue. Simply ask your assigned representative via Telegram and they will provide the relevant documentation prior to purchase. We believe full transparency is fundamental to research integrity.
Our minimum accepted purity is 98.5%. Most products in our catalogue exceed 99.0% purity. Any batch that falls below our threshold is rejected and not listed. Exact purity for each product is documented in the CoA.
Lyophilised (freeze-dried) peptides should be stored at -20°C in a sealed, dry container, away from light and moisture. Once reconstituted in bacteriostatic water, peptides should be stored at 4°C and used within 30 days. Always follow the specific storage instructions included with your order. Avoid repeated freeze-thaw cycles.
