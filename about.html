About OzzyPeps

Our Story

Built for the Research Community

OzzyPeps was founded by a team passionate about advancing scientific research in Australia. Frustrated by inconsistent quality, slow fulfilment, and a lack of transparency from existing suppliers, we set out to create a different kind of peptide company.

Every compound we offer is sourced from GMP-compliant manufacturers and independently tested before it reaches your lab. We believe researchers deserve to know exactly what they're working with — that's why every product comes with a Certificate of Analysis.

We operate with a small, knowledgeable team reachable directly on Telegram. You'll never be left waiting on a ticket queue — just quick, informed answers from people who understand the compounds they're supplying.

Our Process

From Manufacturer to Your Lab

1

GMP-Compliant Sourcing

We source exclusively from manufacturers operating under Good Manufacturing Practice standards, with full documentation of synthesis protocols and raw material provenance.

2

Independent Laboratory Testing

Every batch is sent to a certified third-party laboratory for HPLC purity analysis and mass spectrometry confirmation of molecular identity before it enters our inventory.

3

Cold-Chain Storage

Approved compounds are lyophilised and stored at -20°C in our temperature-controlled facility to maintain stability and prevent degradation prior to dispatch.

4

Discreet Dispatch

Confirmed orders are packed in plain, unmarked packaging with desiccant where required, and dispatched promptly with a tracking number provided on confirmation.
